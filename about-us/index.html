Our story

Founded in 1986.
Still here for a reason.

Phyllis Sullo opened the doors on North Federal Highway with one chair and one standard: do the work right, take care of the person in front of you. Nearly four decades later, that address hasn’t changed.

Today Heather Marino and Matteo Spatafora carry the salon forward. Both Goldwell Master Colorists. Both still behind the chair, still consulting before every appointment — the way it’s always been done here.

Color, balayage, extensions, and full-service spa. All under one roof in Fort Lauderdale. No trends chased, no shortcuts taken.
