About Sullo

39 years on North Federal. One standard: exceptional.

Sullo Salon & Day Spa was founded by Phyllis Sullo on North Federal Highway in Fort Lauderdale in 1986. What started as one woman’s commitment to doing great work has grown into a 39-year institution — one that Glamour named among the ten best salons in the country.

Today the salon is carried on by master stylists Heather Marino and Matteo Spatafora, both with over 40 years behind the chair. Their approach hasn’t changed: the same clients, the same hands, the same standard. Fort Lauderdale’s most trusted salon isn’t a franchise or a rotating roster. It’s a place where your colorist knows your hair better than you do.

Sullo is a Goldwell Platinum color salon, Great Lengths and Olaplex certified, and home to some of the most experienced master colorists in South Florida. 4.5 stars across 93 Google reviews — most from clients who have been coming for a decade or more.
